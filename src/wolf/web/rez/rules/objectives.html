In its simplest form Wolf is played by two teams: the Werewolves, whose members know each other's identity, and the innocents (a.k.a. "civilians," "townspeople," "village," "town," etc.), who at first know only the number of wolves in the game.

There are two phases: "night," when the wolves may eliminate a player from the game, and "day," during which players vote to kill a suspect. These phases follow each other continuously until all wolves have been eliminated or the wolves outnumbers the innocents.
